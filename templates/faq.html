What is CodeEx AI?

CodeEx AI is an advanced AI-powered chat platform that provides access to multiple AI providers including OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini, and local AI models.

How do I get started?

Simply create an account using your email or Google account, then start chatting with our AI. No additional setup required!

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use end-to-end encryption for all chat messages and implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

Which AI providers are supported?

We support OpenAI GPT models, Google Gemini, and local AI models. You can switch between providers based on your needs.

Can I export my chat history?

Yes! You can export your chat data from the storage management page in your account settings.

How does the AI learning work?

Our AI system learns from interactions and feedback to provide better responses over time, while maintaining your privacy.

Is there an API available?

Yes! We provide RESTful API endpoints for developers. Check our documentation for more details.

How can I provide feedback?

You can provide feedback directly in the chat interface or contact us through our contact page.
